ABOUT CLEVIRON

Clenviron is here to make people waste disposal hassle free and even get paid or get reward for their waste that can still be recycled.

We believe that littering waste can cause harm to lives and mismanagement of resources as this materials are not useless in the real sense but can be transformed into something reusable and tangible which can also earn us income. We therefore say "Recycling is Life".
